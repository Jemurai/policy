## Gaps

Other NIST Items are unrealistic for small and mid sized companies to achieve.  The are as follows:

* [AC-16](https://nvd.nist.gov/800-53/Rev4/control/AC-16)
* [MP-3](https://nvd.nist.gov/800-53/Rev4/control/MP-3)
